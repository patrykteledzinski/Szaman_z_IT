Who Am I:

My name is Patryk Teledziński. My profile on LinkedIn It reflects my over 16 years of experience in the IT industry, where I worked as a software quality assurance specialist, collaborating with numerous technology companies, mainly in Warsaw, where I come from.

My initial intention in working in IT was to make people's lives easier through technology. Over time, however, I noticed something more – as a tester, I could intuitively sense bugs in software. I also noticed that many of these bugs were the result of the mindset of the people who created them.

The same intuition that helped me work with information systems began to lead me deeper – towards working with the human psyche, emotions, and subconscious.

After the pandemic and the transition to remote work, I felt I wanted to live differently. Instead of "chasing the next career train," I chose quality of life. I moved from Warsaw to the village of Krępa near Piaseczno, where, surrounded by forest, I began to rediscover the simplicity of life – chopping wood, the silence of nature, and running barefoot through the forest year-round.

During this time, I began to delve deeper into the practice of Huna and shamanism, until I naturally became an IT shaman myself – a person who connects the worlds of technology, intuition, and working with human consciousness.

The beginning of my shamanic path was the book "The Urban Shaman" by Serge Kahili King. Thanks to it, I realized that even someone raised in the city can find their shamanic path.

Over time, I also picked up the books "Instant Healing" and "Master Your Hidden Self". I began to practice the techniques described in them and discover the enormous role our subconscious plays in our lives.

When my first questions arose, I found Serge Kahili King's website, where you can participate in weekly online meetings. Since then, I have regularly attended Wednesday meetings, where I have the opportunity to ask questions and learn directly from the author of these teachings.

It's a great honor for me to be able to grow under the influence of his knowledge and experience.

The forest where I walked, practicing Huna and mindwork, became a place of extraordinary encounters for me. One such person was Andrei Lvouski, a shaman from Belarus who practices the banya ceremony.

Our conversations quickly evolved into a regular collaboration. We began to jointly lead banya ceremonies, which take place on weekends.

Thanks to this experience, I can explore this tradition both from a practical and spiritual perspective. Andrei is a teacher – a master – who shows me how to lead ceremonies and how to be a good guide for other people.

A natural extension of my path became working with the human shadow – that is, with those parts of our psyche that have been repressed into the subconscious.

During sessions, I use visualization, intuition, and working with the client's imagination to reach the deep roots of emotional blocks, traumas, and repetitive patterns in their lives.

Many years of working in IT have taught me an analytical approach to problems, and shamanic practice allows me to work with them at the emotional and subconscious level.

Thanks to this, I can help people restore their inner balance, integrate difficult experiences and rediscover connection with their own life force.

If you feel that difficult patterns or emotional blocks are recurring in your life, or you want to get to know yourself more deeply, I invite you to work with me during an individual session.

What does the first session look like?

The first session is a calm and safe process of exploring your history, emotions, and what you're currently experiencing. This isn't therapy in the traditional sense – it's deep work with your subconscious, imagination, and your inner world.

1. Initial consultation
We start with a conversation during which you tell me about your situation and what you want to change in your life.

2. Mental quieting
I introduce you to a state of deep relaxation similar to self-hypnosis, which makes it easier to access your subconscious.

3. Work with imagination and emotions
During the session, we use visualization and your imagination. It is there that images, symbols and emotions often appear, leading us to the root of the problem.

4. Integration and healing
When we discover the hidden parts of your psyche – we work on understanding, accepting and integrating them. This is the moment when true change begins to emerge.

Every session is different because every person has their own unique story. My task is to accompany you on this journey and help you find your own inner strength.

Why people come to the Shaman from IT

People from very different backgrounds come to my work – from people working in corporations, through entrepreneurs, to people on a deep journey of spiritual development.


They most often come up with questions and experiences such as:

• repeating difficult patterns in relationships
• feeling stuck in life or work
• difficult emotions that they cannot understand
• internal blocks and lack of sense of purpose
• desire for deeper self-knowledge

My approach is unique because I combine two worlds:

the world of analytical thinking in IT
and
the world of intuition, imagination and work with the subconscious.

Thanks to this, I can simultaneously understand the logical way of thinking of a modern person and work with what is hidden deeper – in emotions, symbols and the shadow of the psyche.

If you feel that your life is trying to show you something, and you want to understand it – perhaps this is exactly why you have come to this page.

Thanks to her, I learned how to create immediate transformation in my work with clients, increasing the effectiveness of sessions and the depth of the experience.

Mastering Your Hidden Self: Guide to the Huna Way

author: Serge Kahili King

This book teaches you how to discover and utilize the hidden layers of your personality and potential. Practical techniques allow you to understand how our internal mechanisms influence our lives and decisions.

On my journey as an IT Shaman, she helped me understand how to support people in discovering their own power and potential during the DebugowanieCienia sessions.

author: Colin C. Tipping

This book shows how forgiveness can free us from the past and emotional blockages. It teaches us to consciously process difficult experiences and restore harmony in relationships.

After my divorce, this book saved me from being a victim and taught me how to forgive myself and others, which is crucial in my work with clients. My favorite technique from this book is the three lists that help with the process of forgiveness and emotional healing. I often use them as homework for my clients so they can continue their healing between shadowdebugging sessions.
