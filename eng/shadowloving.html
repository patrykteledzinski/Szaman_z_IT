ShadowLoving Sessions:

"Shadows" are parts of our personality that have been repressed into the subconscious and deemed bad, inappropriate, or sinful at some point in our lives. They can remain hidden in the "basement" of our unconscious for many years, unaware of their existence, until illnesses, cyclical life difficulties, or recurring patterns that impede daily functioning arise. For me, emotional pain and life challenges are signposts to contact with these repressed parts. As a shaman experienced in working with the shadow and trauma, I support clients in exploring their inner space – especially through visualizations, imagination, and symbolic images that open access to unconscious aspects of the self. The goal is to know, accept, and love these repressed parts of ourselves, which leads to a fuller life, inner harmony, and real personal growth.

Every session is individually tailored to the needs of the person and their current situation, in which they find themselves. We start with quieting the mind and inducing a state similar to self-hypnosis, which allows people to safely enter the realm of imagination. Unlike a strictly body-based approach, I am guided by visualizations and symbols that appear in the mind. This allows for the discovery of hidden, repressed parts of the self, recognizing the root causes of difficulties, and resolving repetitive patterns. During sessions, I guide people toward integrating these parts—through noticing, accepting, and loving—and then support them in healing their inner child, releasing limiting beliefs, and, if necessary, embarking on conscious spiritual journeys. My goal is to deepen self-awareness, facilitate self-healing, and support people in safely exploring their subconscious world, while fully respecting their individual pace and needs.
